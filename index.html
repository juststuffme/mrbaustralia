For Vendors & Brokers

List for Free.
Always.

MRB Australia is free to list and we intend to keep it that way for individual vendors. The AIMI does the heavy lifting — just tell it where your business is and it builds the listing for you.

  • Give the AIMI a URL, street address or business name — it drafts your full listing description and web tags automatically
  • Upload your Caretaking and Letting Agreements — the system produces a mini legal DD report for vendors and optionally for buyers
  • Upload your Profit & Loss — the AIMI flags any inconsistencies, unusual line items or presentation issues before buyers see them
  • Buyers matched to your listing automatically — the AIMI surfaces it every time a buyer's query fits your metrics
  • Or skip all of that and write your own listing the traditional way — we support both
