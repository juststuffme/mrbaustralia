    For Industry Brokers & Vendors

    List During
    Testing Phase.

    MRB Australia is currently in its initial testing stage. Listings are free during this period. When the platform opens to the public, listing fees will apply. To register your interest now, email broker@mrbaustralia.com and we will notify you when listings are available.

    Broker Listing Standards
    • MRB Australia is open to industry-recognised management rights brokers only. All applicants are verified before listings go live.
    • Duplicate, misleading or questionable listings will result in immediate removal and suspension of access — no exceptions.
    • Listings must be genuine, current and accurately represented. AIMI cross-references metrics against market data and will flag anomalies.
    • MRB Australia also operates an MLR Industry Directory — available only to recognised MLR professionals, solicitors, accountants and industry specialists.
    • Any company or firm that operates in a manner considered a hindrance to the management rights industry must apply for inclusion via a board review by industry peers. The board's decision is final.
    Disclaimer

    AIMI's document analysis is informational only and does not constitute legal or financial advice. Always engage a qualified Queensland solicitor and accountant for formal due diligence.
